How Often Should I Get a Deep Clean?

Every home is different, so there’s no one size fits all answer. It really depends on things like how many people or pets live in your home, how much foot traffic you get, and how often you clean in between.

That said, we usually recommend getting a deep cleaning once or twice a year. A good rule of thumb:

  • check_circle Twice a year for homes with pets, kids, or lots of activity.
  • check_circle Once a year for homes with less mess and traffic.

Doing a deep clean every 6 months, plus regular cleanings in between, will help your home stay fresh all year long.

FAQs: Deep Cleaning

Find answers to the most common questions about our deep cleaning services, scheduling, and satisfaction guarantee.

What’s the difference between deep cleaning and a regular cleaning?

keyboard_arrow_down

Deep cleaning goes beyond surface cleaning and targets neglected areas, while regular cleaning handles daily maintenance.

Will you be deep cleaning my entire home?

keyboard_arrow_down

Yes! All rooms and spaces included in the deep cleaning checklist will be cleaned thoroughly.

Does the deep cleaning include steam cleaning the carpets?

keyboard_arrow_down

Steam cleaning is available as an optional add-on for carpets. Let us know if you want this service.

What is meant by deep cleaning a house?

keyboard_arrow_down

Deep cleaning means thoroughly cleaning every part of the house, including hidden and high-touch areas.

How often should a house be deep cleaned?

keyboard_arrow_down

Typically once or twice a year, depending on household activity, pets, and cleaning habits.

How long does it take to deep clean a house?

keyboard_arrow_down

Usually between 3 to 6 hours depending on the size and condition of the home.

How do I prepare my house for deep cleaning?

keyboard_arrow_down

Remove personal items from surfaces and floors to allow our team to clean thoroughly.

What is the difference between a deep clean and a move-out clean?

keyboard_arrow_down

Move-out cleaning is more intensive, focusing on preparing the home for new occupants, often including appliances and closets.

What is included in the deep cleaning of a house?

High-touch areas, baseboards, blinds, door frames, AC vents, light switches, and optional add-ons like carpet steam cleaning or ceiling fans.
