Why Get Recurring Cleaning Instead of Just a One-Time Cleaning?

Recurring cleaning is the perfect service for keeping your home consistently fresh, healthy, and welcoming. Unlike a one time or basic cleaning, recurring cleaning focuses on maintaining cleanliness over time, preventing the buildup of dust, dirt, and grime before it ever becomes overwhelming.

cleaning_services One Time Cleaning Limitations

A standard one-time cleaning is great for a quick refresh, like wiping down countertops, cleaning floors, and tidying up bathrooms and kitchens. But dust, pet hair, fingerprints, and everyday messes naturally return over days and weeks.

FAQs: Recurring Cleaning

Find answers to the most common questions about our recurring cleaning services, scheduling, and discounts.

What is recurring cleaning?

keyboard_arrow_down

Recurring cleaning is a scheduled cleaning service that happens weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly. It keeps your home consistently fresh, tidy, and sanitized without the hassle of booking every time.

How often should I book recurring cleaning?

keyboard_arrow_down

Most of our clients choose weekly or bi-weekly services to maintain a spotless home. Monthly visits are great for lower maintenance homes or smaller spaces.

What’s included in a recurring cleaning?

keyboard_arrow_down

Our recurring cleaning covers all the essentials: dusting, vacuuming, mopping, wiping counters, bathrooms, kitchens, and general tidying. We keep your home consistently clean so you can focus on what matters most.

Do I need a deep clean before starting recurring cleaning?

keyboard_arrow_down

Yes, we recommend starting with a deep cleaning so your home is thoroughly detailed from the start. After that, recurring cleanings are designed to maintain that level of freshness.

Can I customize my recurring cleaning?

keyboard_arrow_down

Absolutely! We can tailor the cleaning checklist to your preferences—whether you want extra attention on bathrooms, kitchens, or certain rooms each visit.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

keyboard_arrow_down

Not at all. Many of our clients provide us with a key or code so we can clean while they’re out. It’s completely up to you.

What if I need to skip or reschedule a cleaning?

keyboard_arrow_down

Life happens! Just give us notice, and we’ll happily adjust your schedule.

Will I get the same cleaner every time?

keyboard_arrow_down

We do our best to send the same trusted cleaner (or team) for every visit so they get to know your home and preferences.

Do you offer discounts for recurring cleaning?

keyboard_arrow_down

Yes! We reward our loyal clients with discounts: Monthly cleanings: 15% off, Bi-weekly cleanings: 30% off, Weekly cleanings: 40% off.

Is recurring cleaning worth it?

keyboard_arrow_down

Yes! With recurring cleanings, your home stays cleaner for longer, and you save time, energy, and stress. Many clients say it’s one of the best lifestyle upgrades they’ve made.
