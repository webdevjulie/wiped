About WIPED Cleaning Services

Welcome to WIPED Cleaning Services, where cleanliness meets care.

We are a locally owned and operated cleaning company based in Regina, Saskatchewan, dedicated to providing high-quality residential cleaning with a personal touch. At WIPED, we understand how valuable your time and comfort are. That’s why we make it our mission to deliver spotless results and reliable service you can always count on. Whether it’s a deep clean, move-in/move-out, or regular maintenance, we ensure every corner of your home is cleaned to perfection.

Our team is fully trained, insured, and detail-oriented, treating every home as if it were our own. We take pride in using safe and effective products, professional tools, and proven techniques that leave your space refreshed, sanitized, and beautifully maintained. We’re more than just a cleaning service — we’re your partner in creating a home that feels fresh, welcoming, and truly cared for.
